> BIOLOGX is the dark-matter collaboration between Jason Tibi and Richard Toomer — a Brooklyn-based duo > blending post-punk, industrial ambience, and frenetic breakbeats. Their sound emerges from the fog of > disclosure, pulling inspiration from declassified intelligence, UAP phenomenon, and the metaphysics of > remote viewing. > > Richard Toomer (Bed-Stuy, NY), founder of the band Secret Shopper, performs on dual Moog synthesizers, > sculpting thick analog atmospheres. Jason Tibi (Ridgewood, NY), known for solo projects Swan Stalker and > CD Player, drives BIOLOGX’s rhythm section using sequencers, grooveboxes, and disembodied samples. Tibi is > also the founder of Search Results Press, and has worked as a composer on fashion films for Elena Velez > and Weiraen (Shanghai). > > BIOLOGX debuted at Scorpion Records and the DLA Productions warehouse in Brooklyn. Their first EP, > Remote Viewer, will be released in August 2025, preceded by a string of singles. Every performance is a > transmission — a signal from a buried network — demanding disclosure.
